About Casa Cristal

Casa Cristal is situated within one of the most prestigious residential gated communities in Tulum. This post-modern luxury residence offers a perfect combination of practicality and inclusivity, ensuring a comfortable and opulent experience that is impossible to ignore. The property showcases exquisite marble floors and finishes, creating an atmosphere of elegance and grandeur. The addition of granite countertops further enhances its overall allure and sophistication.
